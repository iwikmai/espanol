# Gramatica

### Rules
|Espanol|English|
|:------|------:|
|ser|to be|
|(yo) soy|I am|
|(tu) eres|you are|
|(el/ella/usted) se|you (formal) are|

#### Examples

|Espanol|English|
|:------|------:|
|Soy professor|I am a teacher.|
|¿Eres estudiante?|Are you a student?|
|¿(Usted) es projfessor?|Are you a teacher? (formal)|
|Es medica|He/she is a doctor.|

- The subject pronouns are often omitted
  - e.g. (**Yo**) soy professor
- The formal form 'you' (**usted**) uses the third person singular of the verb

|Masculino|Feminino|
|:--------|-------:|
|**-o**|**-a**|
|camarer**o**|camarer**a**|
|consonante| + **-a**|
|profeso**r**|profesor**a**|
|**-e**|**-e**|
|estudiant**e**|estudiant**e**|
|**-a**|**-a**|
|receptionist**a**|receptionist**a**|

- The word for 'a' (**un/una**) is omitted before the fword for a job or profession:
  - Maria es ~~una~~ camarera
  - Maria es camarera.
